## 🏕️ What is Bazzite Campfire?

Bazzite Campfire is a safe, interactive learning environment for anyone who wants to switch from Windows to Linux – specifically to Bazzite (https://bazzite.gg/). 

Think of it as your personal campfire where you can:
- 🔥 Learn Linux commands in a safe sandbox
- 🤖 Ask Bazzi (your robot friend) anything
- 🎬 Watch tutorials without leaving the page
- 🎒 Collect achievements in your backpack
- 🚀 Prepare for the real Bazzite experience

Everything runs in your browser. Nothing touches your real system. No credit card. No login. Just learning.

## ✨ Features

🖥️ Safe Terminal – Try Linux commands like ls, pwd, whoami – no risk, no real system access

## 🎯 Why Bazzite?

Bazzite (https://bazzite.gg/) is an immutable Linux distribution based on Fedora, designed for gaming and everyday use. It's like SteamOS for your PC – stable, secure, and perfect for Windows refugees.

Reasons to switch:
- ✅ No forced updates – you decide when to update
- ✅ Gaming performance – often better than Windows
- ✅ Immutable = unbreakable – can't mess up your system
- ✅ Open source & free – no spyware, no bloat

## 🗺️ Missions

Mission 1: First Steps – Commands: ls, pwd, whoami – What you'll learn: Basic orientation in the terminal

Mission 2: Files & Folders – Commands: ls -l, ls -a, cd – What you'll learn: Navigating and viewing files

Mission 3: Exploring – Commands: date, cal, echo – What you'll learn: System information and output

Mission 4: Gaming on Linux – Commands: which steam, flatpak list, lutris – What you'll learn: How gaming works on Linux
