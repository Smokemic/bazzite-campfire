🔥 About Bazzite Campfire

Bazzite Campfire is a safe place to learn Linux commands before switching from Windows to Bazzite. Everything runs in your browser – nothing touches your real computer.

Built with 💚 and DeepSeek – by a noob, for all noobs.

🔒 Privacy

We don't collect any data. Everything stays in your browser. No cookies, no tracking, no login. Your progress is saved locally on your computer.

That's it. Simple and private.
